What can I tell you about me?

I’m Rachel, a 27-year-old pharmacist with a wide range of interests and talents. While my professional life revolves around pharmacy, my heart beats to the rhythm of creativity and expression. My journey into the world of emotions through art began at a tender age when I first picked up my grandmother’s old camcorder. Filming life’s moments, both big and small, quickly became a passion. Two years ago, I embarked on an enchanting journey into analog photography. With each click of the camera shutter, I endeavor to capture the raw and unfiltered emotions of life, be it in the midst of lively gatherings or within the serene moments of everyday life. In addition to my love for photography, I’ve also found my heart in music. Two and a half years ago, I took up mixing, using music as a medium to evoke feelings and stir emotions. After honing my skills as a DJ, I’m now stepping into the realm of music production. My unique path is defined by my passion for sharing emotions, and my versatility in expressing them. I believe in the power of connecting with others through shared feelings and experiences. Join me on this journey as I continue to explore and create, weaving together the worlds of pharmacy, filmmaking, photography, and music to celebrate the beauty of life’s emotions.
