My work As A Director

My portfolio encompasses a wide array of projects, each bearing its unique narrative and visual style. « Moments de Vie » allowed me to explore the core of human experiences through simple moments spend with my friends. In my techno party aftermovies, I aim to transport viewers into the heart of the electronic music scene, using vivid visuals and pulsating beats. The festival project « Les Frairies Buissonières », shot with a camcorder, exudes a nostalgic, analog aesthetic, showcasing my versatility as a director. My overarching objective in all of my work is to weave captivating stories and make a lasting impression through the lens of my camera.
